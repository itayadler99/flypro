# FlyPro — 5 Meta Ad Creatives (Round 1)

Faceless brand. All formats designed to run without showing presenter face. Screen recording + voiceover + motion graphics. Hebrew copy. Mobile-first 9:16 + 4:5.

Budget target: ₪10k/חודש, ABO across 5 ad sets (1 creative each), ₪65/day per ad set, scale winners after day 5.

## Launch plan

**Week 1 — Testing phase:**
- 5 ad sets, ₪65/day each (₪325/day, ₪2,275/week)
- ABO budget allocation
- Audience split: AD1+AD3 cold, AD2 LAL, AD4+AD5 retargeting
- Optimization event: Purchase (or Add to Cart if pixel data thin)

**Day 5 evaluation:**
- Kill any ad with 0 purchases AND spend > ₪200 lifetime
- Identify CPA winner (target: <₪90 per purchase = 4x ROAS at ₪397)
- Don't judge ads with <50 conversions yet (learning phase)

**Week 2 — Scale phase:**
- Duplicate winners to CBO at ₪200-300/day
- Add new creative variants of winning angle (3 hooks × 2 lengths)
- Lookalike from buyers when seed > 100

**KPIs to hit week 1:**
- CTR > 1.5%
- Hook rate (3-sec video view) > 30%
- CPC < ₪3
- CPM < ₪40 (Israel)
- CPA target: ₪80-120
